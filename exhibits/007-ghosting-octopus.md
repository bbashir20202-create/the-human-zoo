# Exhibit #007: *Ghostus Octopus*

**Common Name:** The Ghosting Octopus

### Observation
This highly intelligent species suddenly disappears from all communication channels without any explanation, leaving their prey in a state of confusion and self-doubt.

### Natural Habitat
Dating apps, group chats, budding friendships, and professional relationships.

### Mating Call
*Seen typing 3 hours ago*

### Conservation Status
**Thriving**

### Curator's Note
The Ghosting Octopus is remarkable in its ability to vanish without a trace while simultaneously making the other person question their entire personality. One of the cruelest yet most common creatures in the zoo.
