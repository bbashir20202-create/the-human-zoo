# Exhibit #005: *Replygus Mothicus*

**Common Name:** The Reply-Guy Moth

### Observation
This species is helplessly attracted to any woman's post like a moth to flame, always offering unsolicited advice or jokes.

### Natural Habitat
Twitter replies, Instagram comments, and LinkedIn DMs.

### Mating Call
"Actually, I think you'll find..." / "As a man, let me explain..."

### Conservation Status
**Annoyingly Abundant**

### Curator's Note
Despite having no natural predators, this species is widely disliked by every other organism in the zoo.
