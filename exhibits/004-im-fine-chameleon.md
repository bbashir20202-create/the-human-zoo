# Exhibit #004: *Fineus Emotionalis*

**Common Name:** The "I'm Fine" Chameleon

### Observation
This creature is slowly dying inside but maintains perfect camouflage by repeating "I'm fine" with a smile.

### Natural Habitat
Family group chats, workplace Slack, and therapy waiting rooms.

### Mating Call
"I'm fine", "It's nothing", "Don't worry about me"

### Conservation Status
**Critically Endangered (but won't admit it)**

### Curator's Note
Perhaps the most common and tragic exhibit in the entire zoo. Everyone has seen one. Many have been one.
