# Exhibit #008: *Mourningus Performatus*

**Common Name:** The Social Media Mourning Bird

### Observation
This species remains completely silent while someone is alive, but becomes extremely vocal and emotional the moment they die — posting long tributes, old photos, and "You will be missed" captions.

### Natural Habitat
Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter/X timelines after someone passes away.

### Mating Call
"Can't believe you're gone... you were such an inspiration" (posted 3 years after last speaking to them)

### Conservation Status
**Extremely Common**

### Curator's Note
One of the most performative animals in the zoo. Interestingly, their mourning volume is often inversely proportional to how close they actually were to the deceased.
