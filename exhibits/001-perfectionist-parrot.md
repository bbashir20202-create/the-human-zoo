# Exhibit #001: *Perfectionistus Paralysis*

**Common Name:** The Perfectionist Parrot

### Observation
This species spends 6 hours editing a 3-line message, then still doesn't send it.

### Natural Habitat
Group chats, email inboxes, Notes app, and "Launch soon" Twitter accounts.

### Mating Call
"I'll share it when it's ready" / "It's not perfect yet"

### Conservation Status
**Critically Common**

### Curator's Note
Often seen in the wild saying "I'm my own worst critic" while simultaneously being paralyzed by that same criticism.
