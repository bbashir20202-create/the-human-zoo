# The Human Zoo Manifesto

We are all animals.

We like to believe we are rational, civilized beings.  
But we doomscroll at 3am.  
We say "I'm fine" while dying inside.  
We humblebrag. We ghost. We chase dopamine. We lie to ourselves.

This repository is not here to shame humanity.

It is a **zoo**.

A place where we can finally look at ourselves clearly — without filters, without performance, without the need to appear better than we are.

Every exhibit is a mirror.

Some exhibits will make you laugh.  
Some will make you uncomfortable.  
Some will feel like someone wrote about *you*.

That is the point.

We are not above the animals.  
We *are* the animals.

And perhaps, by seeing ourselves in these cages, we can finally begin to understand our own behavior.

**Rule #1:** No one is above the zoo.  
**Rule #2:** If you recognize yourself in an exhibit — welcome. You are home.

This is The Human Zoo.

Come. Observe. Reflect.

And maybe, just maybe… evolve.
